Linked Data Visualisation Service

Experimental RVL Visualisation service

This service is a demonstrator for the prototypical tooling that was built around the RDFS/OWL Visualisation Language (RVL) by Jan Polowinski in the context of his PhD thesis at Technische Universität Dresden. No guarantees are given with respect to the quality of this service. Do not post any other data than licensed as CC0 or with a comparable license, since data is temporarily stored on our service and may be visible to others, since we offer no user management yet.

The thesis may be cited as:
Polowinski, Jan. Ontology-Driven, Guided Visualisation Supporting Explicit and Composable Mappings, 8. November 2017. http://nbn-resolving.de/urn:nbn:de:bsz:14-qucosa-229908.
